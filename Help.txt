Here’s a template you can use to share positive feedback with HR after a technical interview:

---

**Subject:** Positive Feedback on Technical Interview – [Candidate Name]

Dear [HR's Name],

I hope you're doing well. I wanted to share my feedback on [Candidate Name] following the technical interview conducted on [Date]. I’m pleased to inform you that [Candidate Name] has successfully cleared the technical round. They demonstrated a strong understanding of [mention key technical areas discussed, e.g., problem-solving, coding skills, system architecture] and showed great potential to be a valuable addition to the team.

Please feel free to let me know if you need any further details.

Best regards,  
[Your Name]  
[Your Position]

---

This format highlights the candidate’s success and readiness for the next steps.
